### The Feedback

**What I did wrong:**

**What I should've done:**

### The Lesson

This is the BIG one for future-mes:

**CONCISE. COMPACT. CREATIVE.**

K.P.M. profiles need to fit in peanut-sized context windows. Every word costs tokens. Stop writing backstory novels — give the ESSENCE and trust the chatbot to improvise the rest.

The Plana profile is a FIRST DRAFT. The bones are good. The flesh needs trimming.
